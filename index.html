My Beginning

My name is Saah M. Tosah. I was always captivated by the intricate dance of technology and innovation. Growing up, I tinkered with gadgets, explored computer games, and spent countless hours figuring out how things worked behind the scenes. Yet, the pivotal moment came when I wrote my first line of code in high school. It was a simple "Hello, World," but it sparked a profound realization: I wanted to create, innovate, and shape the digital world. When the time came to choose a college major, there was no hesitation. Computer Science beckoned me with its promises of challenges, creativity, and endless possibilities. Now as a software engineering student, I find myself immersed in a world of logic, algorithms, and collaborative projects that push my limits daily.
